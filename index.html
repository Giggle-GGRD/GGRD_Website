Fair-launch path

How the Meteora DBC Launch Works

GGRD uses a public Dynamic Bonding Curve (DBC) on Meteora. There is no presale. Everyone joins under the same rules directly on-chain.

  1. Prepare a Solana wallet (for example Phantom or Backpack).
  2. Fund it with SOL and USDC on Solana.
  3. When the fair launch begins, the official DBC link and token address will be published here and on @GGRDofficial and @ContactGiggle.
  4. You will be able to buy GGRD directly from the DBC using USDC until the migration threshold (around 80,000 USDC in curve TVL) is reached.
  5. After migration, liquidity is moved to a standard AMM pool (for example on Jupiter / Raydium) and trading continues there with no tax.
Important: Until the official contract and DBC addresses are published on this website, any token claiming to be “GGRD” should be treated as a scam. Always double-check addresses via this site and the official communication channels.

Charity Treasury

10% Supply for Ukrainian Children with Cancer – Tabletochki Only

The GGRD Charity Treasury is a dedicated on-chain wallet holding 100,000 GGRD (10% of total supply). It is controlled by a 3/4 Squads multisig and has one exclusive purpose: funding humanitarian aid for Ukrainian children with cancer and their families, especially those affected by war.

Under the Donation Policy, the only beneficiary of this treasury is Tabletochki Charity Foundation. Funds may not be used for the team, marketing, liquidity, CEX fees or any profit-oriented activities. The treasury can only:

  • swap GGRD into assets accepted by Tabletochki (for example USDC),
  • send on-chain donations to the official wallets or payment gateways of Tabletochki.

All donations are executed via the dedicated e-mail address charity@ggrd.me and the official multisig:

Charity Treasury multisig: C96zqEjGh22yuZknVrasLyr2j5b2xsMnbTUiu2WKLK9w

Planned 10-Month Donation Schedule

After the DBC migrates to a standard LP and trading stabilises, the Charity Treasury follows a staged, roughly 10-month programme:

  • each month a defined portion of GGRD is swapped to USDC,
  • swaps respect strict daily and monthly volume limits versus average trading volume,
  • at the end of each month, a single on-chain donation is sent to Tabletochki,
  • all transactions and confirmations are published in public transparency reports.
Transparency: the project regularly publishes on-chain proofs of swaps and donations, linking every payment to the Charity Treasury multisig and Tabletochki’s official infrastructure.
Disclaimer: Tabletochki Charity Foundation does not promote, manage or recommend the GGRD token or any related products. All donations are unilateral decisions of the GGRD project.
Security & risk

No Hidden Tax, No Fee Switch, No Blacklists

GGRD is designed to behave like a standard, transparent SPL token:

  • no built-in buy or sell tax; trading tax is 0%,
  • no owner-controlled fee switch or privileged fee wallet,
  • no hidden blacklisting, mint or freeze functions for users,
  • token authorities are configured according to the security section of the whitepaper (for example: mint authority revoked after launch, freeze authority set to none, metadata authority migrated away from a single EO wallet).
DYOR: always verify the token mint address using the official links on this website and compare it with data from Solscan and Meteora before you interact with any contract that claims to be GGRD.
