ü¶ä Giggle Reloaded ($GGRD) ‚Äì Technical Overview

GGRD is a fair-launch Solana memecoin with a fixed supply of 1,000,000 tokens. It combines high-energy meme culture with a transparent, on-chain charity mechanism for children with cancer, executed via community-selected verified charitable organizations.

Launch is conducted through a Meteora Dynamic Bonding Curve (DBC) using USDC as quote asset, with migration to an LP pool near an 80,000 USDC technical threshold. 40% of supply is sold on the curve, while 60% is distributed between dedicated multisig treasuries.

Security architecture includes 0% trading tax, no freeze authority, no presale or VC allocations, and 3/4 Squads multisigs controlling treasuries and metadata. All details, including the full Donation Policy and Charity Treasury rules, are described in the document.
